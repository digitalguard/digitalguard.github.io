TrustLabs unique solution address businesses needs for weaving identity into apps and websites

  1. Social Login - for businesses that value minimum authentication friction, social login offers a quick way of on-boarding your customers. 
  2. eID - for businesses with use cases requiring higher level of  identity assurance, electronic identity (eID) schemes like BankID, & MitID offer stronger verification, KYC and AML support. (coming soon)
  3. Self-Issued OpenID Provider - for businesses concerned about users privacy and data leaks TrustLabs offer support for Self-Issued OpenID Provider. (coming soon)
  4. Self-Sovereign Identity adapter -  for businesses requiring minimum authentication friction, high level of assurance of customer identity while preserving customers privacy rights. (coming soon)
